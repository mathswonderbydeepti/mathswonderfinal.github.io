About MathsWonder

Concept-Based Math Tutoring for Indian and NRI Families

MathsWonder is a live, interactive online math tutoring service for students in Grade 3–12 based in the USA. Founded and personally taught by me, a qualified math educator with over 20 years of teaching experience and more than 15,000 hours of tutoring, MathsWonder specialises in concept-based math learning delivered through live online classes.

Unlike large tutoring franchises, MathsWonder is built specifically for Indian and NRI families in the USA who want the mathematical rigour of India's education tradition combined with full alignment to the US Common Core State Standards for Mathematics.

Every session is taught personally by me — there are no substitute tutors, no rotating instructors, and no marketplace teachers. Your child always learns from the same trusted expert.
